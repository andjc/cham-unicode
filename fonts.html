body {
  font-family: 'Noto Sans Cham', sans-serif;
}

Open Institute fonts

Open Institute have provided three opensource fonts using the Western Cham variety of the Cham script. These fonts contain additional Cham characters that are only used in Cambodia. These additional characters have not been encoded in Unicode yet, but proposals are under development to included these characters in Unicode.

  • Cham OI_Kul
  • Cham OI_Kulbleng
  • Cham OI_Tangin
